Personal Interests

Outside of my academic pursuits, I have a wide range of interests that keep me active and engaged. You might catch me on the courts playing volleyball with my IM team, a hobby of mine since I discovered it in high school. I’m a big gamer as well; I love the mind games of a competitive Pokemon battle or Super Smash Bros bout. Food and music are two of my other great passions. I love trying out all of the amazing restaurants that Berkeley has to offer, and discovering new culinary delights. Music, on the other hand, has been a constant companion throughout my life. I have been playing the piano for fun for many years, and I find it to be a wonderful outlet for creativity and expression. Lately, I have a slight obsession with Settlers of Catan. Poker nights are also a regular occurrence in my circle of friends. While the strategic thinking is engaging, my favorite part of the game is the social aspect.
